An Accounts Payable team member role at PlaceMakers, responsible for accurately processing invoices and credit notes to support branch operations across New Zealand. The role is transactional in nature and suited to someone building their career in a corporate finance environment. Day-to-day tasks are high-volume AP processing.

View details

PlaceMakers is one of New Zealand's largest building materials retailers, part of the Fletcher Building Group, with a strong national branch network.

The Role:
Looking to build your career in a fast-paced corporate finance environment? This is a great opportunity to join our Accounts Payable team, where you’ll support our branch network across New Zealand and work closely with suppliers to ensure invoices and credit notes are processed accurately and on time.

Daily you will be responsible for, however not limited too - 
  • Process of supplier invoices and credit notes accurately and on a timely basis
  • Complete supplier statement reconciliations and resolve discrepancies
  • Respond to supplier and branch queries in a timely and professional manner
  • Maintain supplier records and support compliance with internal processes
  • Processing internal adjustments (when required) and assisting in non-stock PO matching
  • Support continuous improvement initiatives and best practice across Accounts Payable 
  • Provide general administrative support and assist the AP Manager as needed

This is a permanent role offering 40 hours a week, Monday-Friday, based at our Support Office in Penrose. 

About you:  
You’re someone who enjoys working at pace and thrives in a collaborative team environment. You take pride in your accuracy, communication, and ability to solve problems

We’re looking for:
  • Experience in a high-volume Accounts Payable or finance admin environment
  • Strong numerical, reconciliation, and data processing skills
  • Confidence using ERP systems (JDE experience is a bonus) and MS Office
  • Professional written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and organisational skills
  • A positive, proactive attitude and strong team focus
  • The ability to manage priorities and meet deadlines
  • Adaptable, flexible and strong team player 

Our Benefits:
  • Competitive salary & Staff-buying privileges 
  • Genuine development opportunities that come with being part of the wider Fletcher Building Group
  • Employment Education Fund to support your learning
  • Health & Wellbeing initiatives including an Employee Assistance Program 
  • A supportive, inclusive team where you can grow your career
